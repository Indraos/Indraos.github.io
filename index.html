Hi, I am Andy. I am a Ph.D. Candidate at MIT. I design and analyse computational institutions such as recommender systems, and repricers in ecommerce. I use Microeconomic modelling (in particular mechanism design) to make these systems more efficient, transparent and equitable.

This blog has three categories of posts: "recommending" features my work on recommender systems; "allocating" features my work on distributed learning and market design, "learning" shows my analyses of machine behavior, studied from an Economic lense.
