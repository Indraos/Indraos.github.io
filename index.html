Hi, I am Andy. I design computational institutions such as recommender systems, federated learning systems, or automated exchanges between machines as social institutions. I use Microeconomic modelling (in particular mechanism design) to make these systems more efficient, transparent and equitable. Currently, I am a Ph.D. Candidate at MIT.

This blog has three categories of posts: "recommending" features my work on recommender systems; "training" features my work on distributed learning, "learning" shows my analyses of machine behavior, studied from an Economic lense.
